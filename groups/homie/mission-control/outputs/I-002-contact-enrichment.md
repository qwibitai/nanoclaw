### Key Observations

1. **National Builders with CA Divisions**: Most major homebuilders (Lennar, D.R. Horton, PulteGroup, Taylor Morrison, Meritage) have dedicated California divisions with local contact information that may differ from corporate headquarters.

2. **Master-Planned Community Developers**: FivePoint and The Irvine Company are the primary master-planned community developers in coastal California and are key contacts for large-scale development projects.

3. **Regional Specialists**: San Joaquin Valley Homes focuses exclusively on Central Valley development; Williams Homes is strongest in LA County and Central Coast.

4. **Acquisitions**: CalAtlantic Homes (formerly Standard Pacific) was acquired by Lennar in 2018 - contact Lennar directly for former CalAtlantic projects.
