## Summary Notes

### Key Findings:
- **Tier 1 builders** (Lennar, D.R. Horton, Pulte, Taylor Morrison, Meritage, KB Home) dominate CEQA filings as applicants for residential subdivisions
- **FivePoint Holdings** and **The Irvine Company** are the largest master-planned community developers and have extensive CEQA litigation history
- **Williams Homes** is notably active in CEQA litigation in LA County and Central Coast regions
- **Affordable housing developers** (Related California, MidPen Housing) frequently appear in CEQA filings for affordable housing projects
- **National general contractors** appear more as construction partners on CEQA projects rather than lead applicants

### Methodology:
- Companies ranked by frequency of appearance in CEQA project documents (EIRs, MNDs, Notices of Determination)
- Search sources: CEQAnet database, California county planning CEQA notices, CEQA litigation case databases
- Information gathered from web searches of CEQA filings, developer websites, and land use legal databases

### Data Gaps:
- This list represents publicly visible CEQA applicants; private developers and smaller regional builders may be underrepresented
- CEQA project frequency fluctuates with housing market cycles and development activity
- Some companies may appear under different legal entity names in official filings
