# Microgrid Foundry

Microgrid Foundry (MGF) is an energy community Operator based in the UK, operated by Damon Rand. They manage community energy schemes including solar installations, battery storage, and EV charging infrastructure.

## Communities

### Water Lilies Community Energy (WLCE)
- Location: Bristol
- 33 homes with community battery and solar
- Battery trading via Axle Energy on wholesale markets
- Uses Simtricity Flows for meter data collection
- Uses Simtricity Flux for battery dispatch control

### Hazelmead Community Energy (HMCE)
- Location: Bridport, Dorset
- 54 homes with community battery and solar
- Battery trading via Axle Energy on wholesale markets
- Uses Simtricity Flows for meter data collection
- Uses Simtricity Flux for battery dispatch control

## Key Facts
- Both sites are live and operational, trading on wholesale electricity markets daily
- Microgrid Foundry handles day-to-day operations, customer service, and ongoing support
- The sites operate as license-exempt electricity suppliers under UK regulations
- Cepro (sister company) developed and constructed both sites
- Simtricity provides the software platform (Flows, Flux, Nexus)
