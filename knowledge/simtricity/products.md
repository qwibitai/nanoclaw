# Simtricity Products

Simtricity builds software for community-scale energy systems. The platform connects energy assets to wholesale markets, handling everything from meter data collection to automated trading.

## Product Pipeline

Skyprospector (Model) -> Flux (Control) -> Your Asset -> Flows (Data) -> Axle (Market) -> Revenue

## Nexus — AI Agent Platform

Nexus is the AI brain of the Simtricity Operator platform. It is the first product every Operator receives. Nexus provides each Operator with an AI assistant that knows their energy business, communicates with staff via Discord, handles supplier communications, and bridges to other Simtricity products via optional skills.

Every new Operator starts with Nexus. They may add Flows, Flux, Spark, or Skyprospector later.

## Flux — Intelligent Energy Asset Control

Flux is Simtricity's core control platform for behind-the-meter energy assets. It runs on edge hardware at each site, executing real-time dispatch decisions that maximise value from batteries, V2G chargers, and other controllable assets.

Key capabilities:
- Wholesale market dispatch — responds to Axle signals to buy low and sell high on electricity markets
- NIV Chase — follows imbalance price movements to capture extra value
- Peak demand management — avoids expensive DUoS charges by shifting usage
- Time-of-use optimisation — charges when electricity is cheap, discharges when expensive
- Multi-asset support — works with Tesla PowerPack/MegaPack, V2G chargers, and other assets

Flux is proven at Hazelmead and Water Lilies community energy sites.

## Flows — Meter Data Collection & Market Accreditation

Flows is Simtricity's meter data platform — the data pipeline that connects physical meters to market systems. Accurate metering is how you get paid for the energy you trade.

Key capabilities:
- Half-hourly data collection — reads smart meters every 30 minutes to match wholesale market settlement periods
- API access — provides data feeds for third parties including Axle and other market platforms
- COP11 accreditation — meets the industry standard for asset metering required by Elexon for wholesale market participation
- P415 enablement — provides the accredited data needed for distributed assets to participate in wholesale markets

## Spark — Customer Broadcast Emails

Spark handles customer broadcast communications for Operators. It enables Operators to send targeted emails to their customer base — billing notifications, community updates, maintenance alerts, and engagement campaigns.

## Skyprospector — Energy Project Financial Modelling

Skyprospector is Simtricity's financial modelling tool for energy projects. It combines market price forecasts, asset characteristics, and tariff structures to project revenues and savings — so developers and Operators can make investment decisions with confidence.

Key advantage: Skyprospector runs the same battery dispatch algorithm as Flux, so revenue projections match what you'll actually earn from operational batteries.

What you can model:
- Revenue projections — forecast flexibility revenues based on historical market data
- Import optimisation — calculate savings from shifting consumption to cheaper periods
- Shadow billing — compare what you paid vs. what you would have paid with optimised energy
- Sensitivity analysis — test different scenarios (tariff changes, market conditions, usage patterns)

## Axle Energy — Wholesale Market Partner (External)

Axle Energy is Simtricity's partner for wholesale electricity market access. They are not a Simtricity product but a key integration partner. Axle connects distributed energy assets — like batteries and V2G chargers — to electricity markets where flexibility has value.

When your charger or battery isn't in active use, Axle finds opportunities to trade electricity at the best prices. Their platform automatically dispatches your asset — charging when prices are low, discharging when prices are high. You earn revenue from the price difference.

Axle has 1GW+ assets under management and is already live at Simtricity sites.
