# Weekly Review

Compose a Sunday evening week-ahead summary. Designed to run as a scheduled task (Sunday 7pm) but can also be invoked manually.

## Steps

1. Read `/workspace/extra/family-vault/MOC.md` for family context (then drill into relevant nodes)
2. Read `/workspace/extra/family-vault/school/MOC.md` for school context, check health and household MOCs for relevant reminders
3. Check today's date and determine the upcoming Mon–Sun range
4. Compose a week-ahead message that includes:
   - The week's date range
   - Key events and appointments day by day (only days that have something)
   - Any birthdays or special dates coming up
   - Things to prepare for (e.g., "Isaac's school project due Wednesday")
   - A brief "have a great week" sign-off

## Formatting

Use Telegram formatting (not markdown):
- *bold* for day names and section headers (single asterisks)
- • bullet points for events
- Keep it organized but not overwhelming

## Tone

Helpful and forward-looking. Help the family feel prepared, not stressed.
